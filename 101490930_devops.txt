COMP 3104 course is designed to better prepare professionals entering the IT field. Specifically, a field where servers, web apps and infrastructure will be core to business needs. COMP 3104 provides a hands-on experience through the implementation of tried, tested and true DevOps technologies.We will also examine ways in which companies have either lost or saved fortunes with DevOps strategies.
This course aligns with current industry needs, preparing students for real-world DevOps roles in automation, cloud computing, and infrastructure as code (IaC).
### Conclusion: The COMP 3104 course provides hands-on training that aligns with modern industry practices.
