DevOps plays a crucial role in the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) by integrating development and operations teams.
By implementing Continuous Deployment and Automated Testing, DevOps helps in delivering reliable software faster.
Organizations adopting DevOps see improvements in code quality, reduced deployment failures, and faster recovery from production issues.
DevOps reduces the risk of deployment failures and improves recovery time.
DevOps integrates automation into every phase of software development.
Security practices in DevOps help prevent vulnerabilities early in the SDLC.
