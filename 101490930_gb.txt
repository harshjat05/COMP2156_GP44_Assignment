George Brown College, located in Toronto, Ontario, offers over 160 career-focused certificate, diploma, and degree programs across a variety of disciplines. The college emphasizes practical, hands-on learning, giving students valuable experience to succeed in their chosen fields. With strong industry connections, George Brown provides extensive opportunities for internships, co-ops, and networking. It's an inclusive institution, welcoming students from diverse backgrounds and fostering a vibrant community.George Brown College has three main campuses in downtown Toronto: the Casa Loma Campus, the St. James Campus, and the Waterfront Campus. The college offers over 200 programs, including apprenticeships, diplomas, degrees, and continuing education courses.
