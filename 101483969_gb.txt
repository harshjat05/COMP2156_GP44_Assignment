George Brown College’s Casa Loma Campus is one of its main campuses in Toronto, located at 160 Kendal Avenue. Nestled near the scenic Casa Loma, this campus primarily focuses on engineering technologies, construction, and skilled trades programs. It is well-equipped with modern labs, workshops, and industry-standard facilities to provide hands-on learning experiences.
1. Location – A well-known public college in Toronto, Canada with three main campuses: Casa Loma, St. James, and Waterfront.
